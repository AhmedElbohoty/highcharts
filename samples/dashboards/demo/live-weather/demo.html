This demo shows weather forecasts from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute. The data contains the current observation and forecast for the next 24 hours. The data shown is from the largest marker on the map and by clicking on another marker the corresponding forecast is displayed.

This demo contains four different components:

  • Highcharts Maps
  • Dashboard KPI (Key Performance Indicator)
  • Dashboard Datagrid
  • Highcharts spline chart
